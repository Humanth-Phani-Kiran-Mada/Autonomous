## 💡 Key Innovations

### 1. True Autonomy
Unlike typical AI systems that require external goals and feedback, this system generates its own goals based on intrinsic motivation (curiosity, competence, autonomy, mastery).

### 2. Principled Uncertainty
Uses Bayesian inference instead of ad-hoc heuristics for decision-making under uncertainty. All estimates include confidence intervals.

### 3. Meta-Level Learning
Learns not just facts, but how to learn. The system improves its own learning strategies based on what works best in each domain.

### 4. Self-Awareness
Maintains an explicit self-model comprehensively:
- Knows its capabilities and their levels
- Knows its limitations and creates mitigations
- Knows how it thinks (introspection)
- Knows how it's improving (meta-learning metrics)

### 5. Resilience
Multiple error recovery strategies ensure the system keeps learning even when individual approaches fail. Learns from every error.

### 6. Knowledge Preservation
Using spaced repetition (Ebbinghaus curves), the system prevents catastrophic forgetting - a major problem in typical neural networks.
